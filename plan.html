Goals

Slammers Beach Volleyball is a small local business. In creating a website for their business they are wanting to improve their business takings. With the launch of their new website they want to achieve an increase of 50% in their customers.

Success Evaluation

For the new Slammer's Beach Volleyball website to be considered a success, after it has been established, if the number of people playing each week has increased from 100 to +150, the website will be considered a success. It would have achieved it's goal of increasing the business's customers by at least 50%.

Target Audience

The website is intended for a younger audience, of those in the 15-30 years age group. Best description of the demographic would be local students. The client has stated that they want to get kids playing while they're in high schoool in the hopes of retainng players for a longer period of time.

As this website is intended for young students, the website will be designed to be simple so that at a glance they will know what each page is for and how to navigate to the next page they want to be on. Also to keep their attention there will be a visual element on each page i.e. an image.
